Deity payments are processed through our payment service.

This handles interactions between different payment providers so you don't have to.

## Features

- Multiple payment providers
- Country, currency and cart total based method / provider filtering
- Web payments (Apple & Google Pay).
- Stored payment methods / wallet
- Refunds (full & partial)

## PSPs

Our list of supported payment providers is always growing. We currently support:

- Adyen
- Mollie
- HUMM
- Stripe
- WPay
