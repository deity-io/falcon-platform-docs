# Composer

Deity is the endlessly flexible commerce composer providing the utmost customer centric approach to any fast-growing and forward thinking ecommerce merchant. Deity Commerce Composer splits your commerce into domain-specific modules. Pick the integrations you want, integrate with other external services, and customize and extend these to fit your needs.
