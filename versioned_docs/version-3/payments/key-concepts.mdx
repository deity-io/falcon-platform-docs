# Key concepts of Deity Payments
<p className="desc pb20">
DPSG (Deity Payment Service Gateway) is a managed and hosted service that handles interactions between Falcon Platform and payment providers.
Next to handling the full payment flow, DPSG is also a way to manage the configuration of your payment providers and methods used by your Falcon App.</p>

## The benefits of using Deity Payments

Integrate with multiple payment service providers to use any payment method. Customize your customersâ€™ payment flows to match their location and preferences.
